Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Plain-language terms. No data sold, no information sent to call centers, no shared bidding pools.

Effective date: May 20, 2026

1. Who we are

West Michigan Bath Audit ("we," "us," "our") is an independent bath remodel audit and specialist-matching service operating in West Michigan. Our mailing address is Grand Rapids, MI. You can reach us at info@westmichiganbathaudit.com or (616) 325-9505.

2. What we collect

When you submit a form on this site, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (optional)
  • Your ZIP code
  • The current condition of your bath (selected from a short list)
  • Anonymous browsing data: visit timestamps, pages viewed, device type, referring URL, and a randomly assigned visitor ID stored in your browser

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, or other sensitive identifiers. We do not ask for them.

3. How we use it

We use your information only to:

  • Contact you to schedule your free bath audit
  • Match you with one vetted specialist in our West Michigan network
  • Send your written audit summary
  • Follow up if your project changes status (and at most once at 90 days if you stop responding)
  • Measure which of our ads and pages are working, so we can improve them

4. Text messages (SMS) and phone calls

By submitting a form on this site, you agree to receive phone calls and text messages from West Michigan Bath Audit at the number you provide. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase — you can request the audit by phone or email instead.

Text STOP to any message to opt out. Text HELP for assistance. To stop calls, ask the team member during your next call or email info@westmichiganbathaudit.com.

Our texts are sent for two purposes only: (a) confirming and scheduling your free bath audit, and (b) delivering your audit summary or a single follow-up if you stop responding. We do not send marketing blasts, promotional offers, or third-party messages.

5. Email

If you provide an email address, we use it to send your written audit summary and any documents related to your bath project. We may also send a short follow-up email if you go quiet during scheduling. We do not add you to a marketing email list, and we do not sell your email to anyone. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email or by writing to info@westmichiganbathaudit.com.

6. Who we share information with

The only third parties who see your contact information are:

  • The one vetted specialist we match you with. They see your name, phone, email (if provided), ZIP, and bath condition so they can reach out and provide a quote. They sign a confidentiality clause with us that prohibits selling, reselling, or sharing your information with anyone else.
  • Our service providers. We use third-party tools to run this site and our business: Notion (lead database), Resend (email delivery), Cloudflare (hosting and security), Meta (advertising), and Google (analytics and advertising). Each handles only the data they need and is bound by their own privacy terms.

We do not sell your information. We do not put you into a multi-contractor bidding pool. We do not pass your information to lead aggregators, call centers, or third-party marketing services.

7. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your form progress, measure how visitors use the site, and show you relevant ads on Meta (Facebook, Instagram) and Google. Specifically:

  • Meta Pixel — tracks which pages you visit and whether you submit a form, so we can measure our Meta ads and show similar visitors more relevant ads.
  • Google Analytics — tracks visit counts, page views, and traffic sources.
  • Visitor ID — a random identifier stored in your browser so we can de-duplicate submissions if you start and finish a form across two visits.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings or opt out of Meta and Google ad personalization through their respective ad-preference dashboards.

8. How long we keep your information

We keep your contact information and audit submission for as long as you remain in our specialist-matching network or for 36 months after your last interaction, whichever is shorter. After that, your record is archived without contact information attached. You can request earlier deletion at any time by emailing info@westmichiganbathaudit.com.

9. Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • See a copy of the information we have about you
  • Correct anything that’s wrong
  • Delete your record
  • Stop all contact (calls, texts, emails)

Email info@westmichiganbathaudit.com with your request and we’ll respond within 7 business days.

10. Security

Your information travels over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection. Our lead database is hosted in Notion with access restricted to authorized team members. Our backend runs on Cloudflare with industry-standard transit and at-rest encryption. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow modern practices and never store payment information (we don’t collect any).

11. Children

This site and our services are intended for homeowners 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted a form, email us and we’ll delete the record.

12. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we’ll update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will also be announced in a notice on our homepage for at least 30 days.
